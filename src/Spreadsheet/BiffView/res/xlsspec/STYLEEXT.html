STYLEEXT: Named Cell Style Extension (892h)

This record is used for new Office Excel 2007 formatting properties associated with named cell styles. As noted previously XFEXT records are only able to handle round-trip formatting when the document was last saved by Office Excel 2007 or later and the formatting has not been changed. This constraint exists because BIFF8 does not have a mechanism for uniquely identifying XF s once they are loaded if document formatting changes. For named cell styles however new formatting properties can be associated with the style XF by name and the style’s formatting can be updated on load (Office Excel 2007 or later).

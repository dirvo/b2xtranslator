HLINKTOOLTIP: Hyperlink Tooltip (800h)

Introduced in Excel 9 (2000) this is a FRT record. It contains the tooltip that appears when the mouse hovers over the hyperlink at the workbook location pointed to by the REF structure. Note : grbitFrt is omitted.
